Current Research Activities

AI2Equity (NIH)

National Institutes of Health · Apr 2024 – Mar 2028

This qualitative sub-study within the AI2Equity initiative examines how patients and healthcare providers perceive cardiovascular risk, access to care, and the role of AI-enabled tools in promoting equitable prevention and treatment. Using a three-round modified Delphi design, the study convenes approximately 170 participants across 17 focus groups to elicit consensus and divergence across stakeholder perspectives.

Findings directly inform the design and evaluation of equitable AI-based cardiovascular risk tools by integrating multi-stakeholder perspectives into model development and implementation.

SaTC:EDU — Cybersecurity Training for Healthcare Professionals (NSF)

National Science Foundation · Aug 2023 – Jul 2026

This project supports the development, deployment, and evaluation of cybersecurity training for next-generation healthcare professionals. Work includes managing instructional delivery across learning platforms, coordinating course logistics, and supporting the collection and analysis of pre- and post-training assessment data.

Findings inform iterative refinement of educational content and support evidence-based improvements to cybersecurity training in health settings.

DREAM-KG / Proto-OKN

National Science Foundation · Oct 2023 – Sep 2026

DREAM-KG develops explainable, adaptive knowledge graphs to address homelessness through AI-enabled decision support. Contributions focus on coordinating the Community Advisory Board and supporting processes that integrate community perspectives into technical development.

Work includes synthesizing qualitative feedback, applying data triangulation methods, and facilitating shared learning between community stakeholders and technical teams to ensure human-centered, equitable system design.

Research Methods & Interests
