Research

I support interdisciplinary research and applied public health initiatives focused on health equity, public health systems strengthening, and responsible, human-centered uses of data and AI.

Current Research Activities

DREAM-KG (NSF)

Develop Dynamic, Responsive, Adaptive, and Multifaceted Knowledge Graphs to Address Homelessness with Explainable AI. I coordinate team workflows, meeting cadence, follow-ups, user feedback activities, and reporting deliverables.

Enhancing Cybersecurity Training for Next Generation Healthcare Professionals (NSF)

Support program coordination, IRB compliance, training logistics, and annual reporting deliverables.

AI2Equity (NIH)

AI integrating social determinants of health to advance health equity in cardiovascular risk prediction. Support protocol development and IRB submissions.

Public Health System Recovery & Resilience (PACT/USAID)

Contribute to training events, partner coordination, timelines, reporting, and logistics supporting public health recovery work.
